Chicken Momos

A delightful Nepali dumpling filled with seasoned chicken

Ingredients:

  • 250g ground chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust according to preference)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 package of momo wrappers (available at Asian grocery stores)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground chicken, chopped cabbage, onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, soy sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and salt. Mix well until everything is evenly incorporated.
  2. Take a momo wrapper and place a spoonful of the chicken mixture in the center.
  3. Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water and fold it over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Press the edges firmly to seal the momo.
  4. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling until all the momos are assembled.
  5. Steam the momos in a steamer basket for about 10-12 minutes, or until the wrappers are cooked through and the filling is cooked.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
